Prosper Factory publishes **educational content** about markets, investing tools, and good decision-making habits.

Our goal is simple: help you build long‑term financial autonomy through clearer frameworks, better tools, and fewer emotional mistakes.

## What you’ll find on this site

- **Tool guides** (e.g., charting, screeners, platforms).
- **Practical checklists** (what to verify before you pay, what to document, what to avoid).
- **Risk warnings** and “limits” sections to keep recommendations balanced.

## Editorial principles

- **No hype, no promises:** we avoid performance claims and focus on process.
- **Actionable structure:** clear sections, steps, and decision criteria.
- **Risk-first mindset:** position sizing and risk management matter more than features.

## Our method

On “reviews” and “comparisons”, our goal is to reduce *practical* mistakes:

1. **Use case scenario:** long-term, active trading, crypto, frequency, currency.
2. **Product type:** underlying asset vs **CFD/derivative**, leverage involvement.
3. **Total cost:** fees + spreads + FX + withdrawals + non-trading fees when applicable.
4. **Withdrawals & friction:** timelines, minimums, support, verification.
5. **Security & custody:** 2FA, email security, and custody tradeoffs (crypto).
6. **Complexity:** complexity increases errors and risk.

We intentionally avoid “magic” promises. A tool can improve your process, but it doesn’t remove risk.

## Updates & corrections

Markets and products evolve. We try to update pages when needed (the “Updated” date appears at the top of articles).

If you notice incorrect or outdated information, contact us via the [contact page](/contact/). We’ll correct issues as quickly as possible.
