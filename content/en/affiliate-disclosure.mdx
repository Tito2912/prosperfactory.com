Some pages on prosperfactory.com may contain **affiliate links**. This means we may receive a commission if you click a link and then make a purchase or sign up, at no additional cost to you.

## What an affiliate link means

An affiliate link is a tracking link that helps attribute signups/purchases to our website. In most cases, it does not change the price you pay, but it helps fund the site.

## Why we use affiliate links

- To support the operation of the website (hosting, tools, content creation).
- To keep most content accessible without paywalls.

## Partners mentioned

Depending on the page, we may recommend (among others):

- **TradingView**: charting/analysis tool (not a broker).
- **Bitpanda**: crypto platform (availability and conditions vary by country).
- **eToro**: multi-asset platform (offering varies by country; some products may involve CFDs).

Each dedicated page includes limitations/risk notes and links to our disclaimer.

## Editorial independence

- We aim to recommend tools based on usefulness and clarity for readers.
- Affiliate relationships do not guarantee that a product will be featured, nor do they change our responsibility to present limitations and risks.

## How affiliate links are presented

Affiliate links typically appear in buttons or “Get started” links. We may also use attributes like `rel="sponsored"` on these links.

## Measurement and privacy

We may measure basic events (for example, CTA clicks) to understand what content is useful. Measurement tools (analytics) load only after consent via our cookie banner.

For details, read our [privacy policy](/privacy-policy/).

## Important risk notice

Investing involves risk. Always review the provider’s fees, terms, and risk warnings on the official website, and read our [disclaimer](/disclaimer/).
