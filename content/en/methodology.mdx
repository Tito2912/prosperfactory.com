Prosper Factory publishes **educational** content. We do not provide personalized investment advice.

## What we evaluate

Our reviews and comparisons focus on **decision criteria**, not “promises”:

- **Product scope:** stocks/ETFs, crypto, CFDs/derivatives, leverage exposure.
- **Total cost:** fees, spreads, FX costs, withdrawals, inactivity fees where applicable.
- **Friction:** onboarding, KYC, deposits/withdrawals, support responsiveness.
- **Safety basics:** account security, 2FA, email hygiene, and custody tradeoffs for crypto.
- **Limitations:** what the product *doesn’t* do (or for whom it’s a bad fit).

## Risk-first approach

Tools can improve execution and reduce mistakes, but they don’t remove risk.

We try to make risk explicit when a product involves:

- leverage,
- derivatives/CFDs,
- custody risk or counterparty risk,
- unclear fee structures.

## How we handle claims, pricing, and updates

Products and pricing can change. We aim to:

- separate **facts** (features, fees as published) from **opinions** (fit for a use case),
- mark uncertainty when needed,
- update key pages when we detect meaningful changes.
