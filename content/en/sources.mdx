We aim to keep content accurate and actionable. Here are the main source types we rely on.

## Provider documentation

- Official product pages (features, limits, regions)
- Fee schedules and pricing pages
- Terms, risk notices, and custody/security disclosures
- Help center articles when relevant

## Public references

- General investing/market education references (risk, leverage, diversification)
- Publicly available documentation about product mechanics
